Stand Up

Milly has been a stand up comedian since 2019, but has been telling jokes and funny stories since she could talk. Pulling anicdotes from her mildly ridiculous life, Milly weaves a hilarious narrative combining tales from her more peculiar encounters (from sex shop drive bys to 3am cake baking sessions) in with observations of 21st century life (the maddening inconsistency of jeans) and adds a splash of the delightfully macabre to offer a unique and side-splitting performance.
