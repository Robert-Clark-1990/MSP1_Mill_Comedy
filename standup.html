Stand Up

Milly has been a stand up comedian since 2019, but has been telling jokes and funny stories since she could talk. Her style has been described as loveably quirky and accidentally intellectual. Pulling anecdotes from her mildly ridiculous life, Milly weaves a hilarious narrative with observations of her encounters - from motorway sex shops to 3am cake baking sessions - and a splash of the delightfully macabre to offer a unique and side-splitting performance.
