About Milly


Yorkshire-born comedian Milly Armstrong Clark started stand up in 2019 as a way to subsidise her Yorkshire Tea addiction, and beguile strangers with her frankly ludicrous life. Her first gig was in York, shortly after she was wrongfully accused of telling a small woman to go back to the circus, so every gig since has been a massive improvement.

When Milly isn't up on stage, she hosts a weekly podcast Tea with the Morale Queen wherein she sits down with a new guest each week and shares positive stories over a good cuppa. Her guests come from all walks of life, and are guaranteed to put a smile on your face!

Ever the Jack of all trades, Milly is also writing a sitcom based on the goings on at a seaside campsite (keep an eye out for it on TV), and is writing a part-autobiographical, part-wild musings book entitled "Tales from under the Carrot Tree", which is due for release in all your local bookstores just as soon as she finishes writing the damn thing.

Milly lives with her husband Rob, a golden retriever called Poppy (or somethings Div dog), two cats named Xena and Kya, and a bearded dragon called Ziggard Christoper Stark Junior (or Ziggy for short). You can keep up to date with all things Milly related by following her on Instagram, Facebook, Twitter or YouTube.
